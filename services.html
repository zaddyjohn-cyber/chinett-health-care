Long-Term Home Care

Chronic conditions require consistent, trusted care. Our long-term matching connects clients with professionals who become familiar faces — building routines, tracking health trends, and becoming an extension of the family.

Continuity is the heart of long-term care. The same dedicated professional learns your preferences, anticipates your needs, and provides the steady presence that makes living with a health condition far more manageable.

This service is ideal for:

  • Chronic illness management
  • Elderly patients needing daily assistance
  • Individuals with disabilities
  • Dedicated nurse assignment
  • Monthly care plan reviews
  • Family communication updates
  • Emergency escalation protocol

  • Regular developmental check-ins

Post-Hospital Recovery

The period after hospital discharge is critical. Rushed recoveries lead to readmissions. Our recovery-focused professionals bridge the gap between hospital and home — ensuring patients heal properly, safely, and comfortably.

We implement your discharge plan to the letter, monitor for complications, and coordinate with your medical team — so recovery happens once, the right way, at home where you heal best.

This service is ideal for:

  • Surgery patients discharged home
  • Stroke or heart attack recovery
  • Orthopedic procedure rehabilitation
  • Discharge plan implementation
  • Wound care & medication oversight
  • Physical therapy coordination
  • 24/7 on-call support in critical phases
