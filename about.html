Our Story

Built on a Belief That Everyone Deserves Exceptional Care

Learn about the people, values, and vision behind Chinett Health Care.

"We didn't start Chinett Health Care to fill a gap in the market. We started it because we saw families struggling to find care they could truly trust — and we knew we could do better."

Chinett Health Care was founded on a simple but powerful conviction: that healthcare should be deeply personal. Behind every care request is a human story — a worried daughter, a recovering father, a child who needs gentleness as much as expertise. We exist to connect those people with professionals who see them as individuals first, and to deliver care that respects their dignity, their preferences, and their lives.

Too often, families navigating healthcare face confusion, stress, and uncertainty. They don't know who to trust, how to verify a professional's credentials, or whether the person entering their home truly cares. We built Chinett to remove that anxiety entirely — taking the time to understand each situation and carefully matching clients with vetted professionals who fit not just the clinical need, but the human one.

But our commitment goes far beyond staffing. We build relationships — staying involved throughout each client's journey, holding ourselves accountable for the quality of every match, and investing in the professionals and communities we serve. When you choose Chinett, you don't just get a nurse. You gain a partner who is genuinely invested in the wellbeing of the people you love.

A Message From Our President

Leading With Purpose

As President of Chinett Health Care, I am committed to ensuring that individuals and families have access to the compassionate, professional care they deserve at every stage of life. This work is deeply personal to me. I have seen, in my own family and in our communities, how the right caregiver can transform not just a patient's health, but a family's entire sense of peace.

When we founded Chinett, I made a promise: that we would never treat care as a transaction. Every match we make is a relationship of trust between a family at their most vulnerable and a professional we have personally vetted. That trust is sacred, and we honour it by holding ourselves to the highest standards in everything we do.

Our approach is simple but uncompromising. We listen first. We understand the medical, personal, and emotional dimensions of each situation. And only then do we match — pairing each client with a professional who brings both genuine clinical excellence and a genuinely caring heart. Anything less is not good enough for the people you love, and it is not good enough for us.

To every family considering Chinett, I offer you this: your loved ones will be cared for as if they were our own. That is the standard I hold our entire team to, every single day — and it is the reason I am so proud of the care we provide.

— Office of the President, Chinett HealthCare Inc.
